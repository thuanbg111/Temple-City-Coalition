About Us

TEMPLE City Coalition (incorporated 2020) is organized under California’s Nonprofit Public Benefit Corporation Law for charitable purposes.

Specific purpose: to support Christopher Elementary School through fundraising, volunteerism, and community engagement that enriches learning and strengthens the school community.

We operate as a recognized 501(c)(3) nonprofit public benefit corporation, EIN 84-3370961. Contributions may be tax‑deductible to the extent allowed by law.

  • Public & charitable purposes (Articles of Incorporation)
  • No private inurement or political campaigning
  • Assets dedicated to educational and charitable purposes
